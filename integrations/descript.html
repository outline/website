Descript is a fun tool for editing audio and video files using text. It is used by podcasters, vloggers, and other types of creators. Outline allows embedding Descript clips.

In an Outline document, simply paste a share link to a Descript public share link and it will be immediately converted into an embedded version where you can play the clip inside of Outline.

This integration works without any additional settings or authentication.
